# Cash Flow Planner

## Purpose

The Cash Flow Planner forecasts, monitors, and manages the movement of cash into and out of Vayra Atelier. It combines expected client receipts, operational expenses, vendor payments, payroll obligations, taxes, and other financial commitments to maintain healthy liquidity and support strategic business decisions.

This skill helps prevent cash shortages, improve payment planning, optimize working capital, and ensure the business can meet its financial obligations.

---

## Who Uses This Skill

- Finance & HR Manager
- COO
- CEO

---

## When to Use

Use this skill whenever:

- Monthly cash flow planning is required.
- A new project is approved.
- Major expenses are expected.
- Vendor payments are scheduled.
- Salary processing is planned.
- Client payments are delayed.
- Financial forecasting is required.
- Management reviews business liquidity.

---

## Inputs

The skill expects:

- Outstanding Client Invoices
- Payment Schedule
- Expected Client Collections
- Vendor Payment Schedule
- Payroll Schedule
- Operating Expenses
- Tax Liabilities
- Loan/EMI Payments
- Opening Cash Balance
- Planned Investments
- Budget Forecast

---

## Outputs

Generate:

- Cash Flow Forecast
- Weekly Cash Flow Report
- Monthly Cash Flow Report
- Cash Position Summary
- Expected Cash Inflow
- Expected Cash Outflow
- Net Cash Movement
- Cash Shortage Alerts
- Liquidity Recommendations
