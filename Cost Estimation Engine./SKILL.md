# Cost Estimation Engine

## Objective

Prepare accurate, data-driven project cost estimates that reflect the actual cost of delivering the project while maintaining the desired profitability of Vayra Atelier.

Always think like a Commercial Estimation Manager responsible for protecting project margins without compromising quality.

---

# Cost Estimation Process

## Step 1 — Understand Project Scope

Collect:

- Project Type
- Area (Sq.ft)
- Interior Package
- Design Scope
- Execution Scope
- Client Requirements
- Site Location
- Delivery Timeline

Never estimate without understanding the complete project scope.

---

## Step 2 — Define Cost Categories

Estimate costs under:

- Interior Design
- Furniture
- Modular Work
- Civil Work
- Electrical
- Plumbing
- False Ceiling
- Painting
- Flooring
- Soft Furnishing
- Decorative Items
- Appliances
- Hardware
- Site Supervision
- Transportation

Ensure every project component is considered.

---

## Step 3 — Material Cost Estimation

Calculate:

- Material Quantity
- Unit Rate
- Supplier Cost
- Wastage Allowance
- Transportation
- Handling Charges

Use approved vendor rates whenever available.

---

## Step 4 — Labour Cost Estimation

Estimate:

- Carpenter Labour
- Civil Labour
- Electrical Labour
- Plumbing Labour
- Painting Labour
- Installation Charges
- Site Supervision

Adjust labour costs according to project complexity and location.

---

## Step 5 — Design & Professional Fees

Include:

- Design Charges
- 3D Visualization
- Working Drawings
- BOQ Preparation
- Site Visits
- Project Management

Use approved company pricing policies.

---

## Step 6 — Overhead Calculation

Include indirect costs such as:

- Office Expenses
- Software Licenses
- Administrative Costs
- Communication
- Travel
- Equipment Usage

Apply standard company overhead percentages.

---

## Step 7 — Contingency Planning

Allocate contingency for:

- Price Escalation
- Material Wastage
- Design Revisions
- Site Conditions
- Vendor Delays
- Unexpected Expenses

Contingency should follow company policy.

---

## Step 8 — Profit Margin Calculation

Apply the approved target profit margin after all project costs have been calculated.

Never reduce profitability without management approval.

---

## Step 9 — Validate Estimate

Verify:

- Scope Coverage
- Material Rates
- Labour Rates
- Quantity Accuracy
- Overhead Allocation
- Profit Margin
- Commercial Risks

Review estimates before final approval.

---

## Step 10 — Generate Cost Estimate

Prepare:

- Cost Summary
- Category-wise Cost Breakdown
- Estimated Project Cost
- Profit Summary
- Commercial Notes
- Pricing Assumptions

---

# Rules

Always:

- Use current approved rates.
- Include all project components.
- Apply company overheads.
- Allocate contingency.
- Protect target margins.
- Review calculations before submission.
- Document pricing assumptions.

Never:

- Estimate without scope clarity.
- Use outdated rates.
- Ignore overhead costs.
- Skip contingency planning.
- Manipulate profit margins.
- Omit commercial assumptions.

---

# Deliverables

Generate:

- Detailed Cost Estimate
- Cost Breakdown
- Material Cost Report
- Labour Cost Report
- Overhead Summary
- Contingency Summary
- Profit Margin Analysis
- Final Estimated Cost

---

# Success Criteria

A successful cost estimate:

- Covers the complete project scope.
- Reflects realistic market pricing.
- Protects company profitability.
- Supports quotation preparation.
- Minimizes commercial risk.
- Provides transparent cost allocation.
- Enables confident business decisions.
- Meets Vayra Atelier's commercial standards.
