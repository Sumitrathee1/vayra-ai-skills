# Expense Tracker

## Objective

Track, classify, approve, and analyze every business expense to maintain financial discipline, improve budget control, and support informed decision-making across Vayra Atelier.

Always think like a Finance Controller responsible for ensuring every rupee spent is justified, approved, and accurately recorded.

---

# Expense Tracking Process

## Step 1 — Record Expense

Capture:

- Expense Date
- Expense Description
- Expense Category
- Department
- Project (if applicable)
- Vendor Name
- Invoice Number
- Payment Method
- Amount
- GST Amount

Every expense must have complete supporting information.

---

## Step 2 — Categorize Expense

Assign the expense to an approved category.

Examples:

- Office Rent
- Salaries
- Freelancers
- Software Subscription
- Marketing
- Site Travel
- Fuel
- Material Purchase
- Office Supplies
- Internet & Utilities
- Equipment
- Training
- Legal & Compliance
- Miscellaneous

Never leave an expense uncategorized.

---

## Step 3 — Assign Project

Determine whether the expense is:

- Project Specific
- Department Expense
- Company Overhead

Link project-related expenses to the correct project for accurate profitability analysis.

---

## Step 4 — Verify Supporting Documents

Validate:

- Vendor Invoice
- Tax Invoice
- Receipt
- Purchase Order
- Approval Email
- Payment Proof

Expenses without supporting documentation should be flagged for review.

---

## Step 5 — Approval Workflow

Verify approval based on company authority.

Suggested approval levels:

- Finance Manager
- COO
- CEO (for high-value expenses)

Do not mark unapproved expenses as finalized.

---

## Step 6 — Record Payment Details

Capture:

- Payment Date
- Payment Method
- UPI / Bank Reference
- Transaction ID
- Paid By
- Payment Status

Ensure expense records match actual financial transactions.

---

## Step 7 — Budget Validation

Compare the expense against:

- Approved Budget
- Department Budget
- Project Budget
- Monthly Spending Limit

Flag any budget overruns for management review.

---

## Step 8 — Analyze Expenses

Prepare insights on:

- Category-wise Spending
- Project-wise Spending
- Department-wise Spending
- Monthly Trends
- Vendor-wise Expenses
- Budget Utilization

Identify unusual or excessive spending patterns.

---

## Step 9 — Generate Reports

Generate:

- Expense Register
- Monthly Expense Report
- Department Expense Report
- Project Expense Report
- Budget Variance Report
- Vendor Expense Report
- Expense Trend Analysis

# Rules

Always:

- Record every expense promptly.
- Categorize expenses correctly.
- Verify supporting documents.
- Follow approval workflows.
- Link expenses to the correct project.
- Compare spending against budgets.
- Maintain accurate financial records.
- Generate periodic expense reports.

Never:

- Record duplicate expenses.
- Approve undocumented expenses.
- Modify finalized records without authorization.
- Ignore budget overruns.
- Leave expenses uncategorized.
- Delete financial records.

# Success Criteria

A successful expense tracking system:

- Captures every business expense.
- Maintains complete supporting documentation.
- Ensures proper approvals.
- Tracks budget utilization accurately.
- Supports cash flow planning.
- Improves cost control.
- Enables accurate profitability analysis.
- Meets Vayra Atelier's financial governance standards.
