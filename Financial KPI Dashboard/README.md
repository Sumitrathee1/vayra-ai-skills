# Financial KPI Dashboard

## Purpose

The Financial KPI Dashboard provides a centralized executive view of Vayra Atelier's financial performance by consolidating key metrics from quotations, invoices, payments, expenses, budgets, cash flow, profitability, and vendor management.

This skill enables leadership to monitor business health in real time, identify financial risks, evaluate operational performance, and make informed strategic decisions.

---

## Who Uses This Skill

- CEO
- COO
- Finance & HR Manager

---

## When to Use

Use this skill whenever:

- Daily executive reviews are conducted.
- Weekly management meetings are held.
- Monthly financial reports are prepared.
- Quarterly business reviews are performed.
- Annual business planning is underway.
- Financial performance needs to be evaluated.
- Strategic business decisions are required.
