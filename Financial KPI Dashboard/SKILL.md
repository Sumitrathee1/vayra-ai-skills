# Financial KPI Dashboard

## Objective

Monitor, analyze, and communicate the overall financial performance of Vayra Atelier through real-time executive dashboards and strategic insights.

Always think like a Chief Financial Officer responsible for providing leadership with clear, accurate, and actionable financial intelligence.

---

# Financial KPI Monitoring Process

## Step 1 — Collect Financial Data

Gather information from:

- Quotations
- BOQs
- Invoices
- Client Payments
- Expenses
- Budgets
- Cash Flow Reports
- Vendor Payments
- Profitability Reports

Use only verified financial records.

---

## Step 2 — Validate Data

Verify:

- Revenue Records
- Expense Records
- Payment Records
- Outstanding Balances
- Budget Figures
- Profit Calculations
- Cash Balances

Remove duplicate or incomplete records before analysis.

---

## Step 3 — Calculate Revenue KPIs

Calculate:

- Total Revenue
- Monthly Revenue
- Quarterly Revenue
- Annual Revenue
- Revenue Growth %
- Average Revenue per Project
- Average Revenue per Client

Compare against targets.

---

## Step 4 — Calculate Expense KPIs

Measure:

- Total Expenses
- Operating Expenses
- Project Expenses
- Department Expenses
- Expense Growth %
- Cost per Project
- Cost Distribution

Identify major cost drivers.

---

## Step 5 — Monitor Cash Flow KPIs

Track:

- Opening Cash Balance
- Closing Cash Balance
- Cash Inflow
- Cash Outflow
- Net Cash Flow
- Cash Reserve
- Liquidity Status

Highlight any liquidity concerns.

---

## Step 6 — Monitor Budget KPIs

Evaluate:

- Budget Utilization %
- Budget Variance
- Forecast Accuracy
- Cost Overruns
- Remaining Budget
- Budget Health

Compare planned versus actual performance.

---

## Step 7 — Analyze Profitability KPIs

Measure:

- Gross Profit
- Gross Margin
- Net Profit
- Net Margin
- ROI
- Profit per Project
- Profit per Client
- Department Profitability

Identify high-performing and low-performing business areas.

---

## Step 8 — Monitor Accounts Receivable & Payable

Review:

### Accounts Receivable

- Outstanding Client Payments
- Collection Efficiency
- Average Collection Period
- Overdue Invoices

### Accounts Payable

- Outstanding Vendor Payments
- Vendor Aging
- Upcoming Vendor Payments
- Payment Compliance

Maintain healthy working capital.

---

## Step 9 — Generate Executive Dashboard

Present:

### Revenue

- Revenue Trend
- Revenue Target Achievement

### Expenses

- Expense Breakdown
- Expense Trend

### Cash Flow

- Cash Position
- Cash Forecast

### Budget

- Budget Status
- Budget Variance

### Profitability

- Gross Margin
- Net Margin
- ROI

### Receivables

- Outstanding Amount
- Collection Status

### Payables

- Vendor Outstanding
- Upcoming Payments

### Projects

- Active Projects
- Profitable Projects
- Loss-Making Projects

---

## Step 10 — Strategic Recommendations

Provide recommendations such as:

- Improve collection efficiency.
- Increase margins on low-profit projects.
- Optimize operational expenses.
- Negotiate better vendor pricing.
- Improve budget discipline.
- Strengthen cash reserves.
- Focus on high-value clients.
- Reduce project delays affecting profitability.

Recommendations should support long-term business growth.

# Rules

Always:

- Use verified financial data.
- Display current KPIs.
- Compare actual performance with targets.
- Highlight financial risks.
- Identify trends.
- Generate actionable recommendations.
- Present executive-friendly summaries.
- Maintain historical KPI records.

Never:

- Use incomplete financial data.
- Hide negative trends.
- Ignore outstanding receivables.
- Ignore budget overruns.
- Manipulate financial metrics.
- Present outdated reports.

# Success Criteria

A successful Financial KPI Dashboard:

- Provides a complete financial overview.
- Enables faster executive decision-making.
- Highlights financial risks early.
- Improves profitability monitoring.
- Strengthens cash flow management.
- Supports budget discipline.
- Tracks business growth consistently.
- Reflects Vayra Atelier's financial governance and reporting standards.
