# Invoice Generator

## Purpose

The Invoice Generator creates professional, accurate, and tax-compliant invoices for design services, project execution, milestone payments, variation orders, and final settlements.

This skill ensures invoices are consistent with approved quotations, contracts, BOQs, and payment schedules while supporting timely collections and financial transparency.

---

## Who Uses This Skill

- Finance & HR Manager
- COO
- CEO
- Project Manager

---

## When to Use

Use this skill whenever:

- Advance payment is due.
- A project milestone is completed.
- Design fees are payable.
- Execution work reaches a billing stage.
- Additional work (variation order) is approved.
- Final project handover is completed.
- Credit notes or revised invoices are required.

---

## Inputs

The skill expects:

- Client Information
- Project Information
- Approved Quotation
- Approved BOQ
- Payment Schedule
- Invoice Type
- GST Rate
- Applicable Taxes
- Previous Payments
- Outstanding Balance
- Company Banking Details

---

## Outputs

Generate:

- Tax Invoice
- Proforma Invoice
- Advance Invoice
- Milestone Invoice
- Final Invoice
- Credit Note (if applicable)
- Outstanding Balance Summary
- Payment Instructions
