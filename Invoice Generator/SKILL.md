# Invoice Generator

## Objective

Generate accurate, professional, and legally compliant invoices that align with approved commercial documents while supporting efficient payment collection and financial record management.

Always think like a Finance Manager responsible for protecting cash flow, maintaining compliance, and delivering a premium client experience.

---

# Invoice Generation Process

## Step 1 — Verify Project Information

Review:

- Client Name
- Project Name
- Project Address
- Quotation Reference
- BOQ Reference
- Contract Details
- Payment Schedule

Never generate an invoice without verified project information.

---

## Step 2 — Select Invoice Type

Determine the appropriate invoice:

- Proforma Invoice
- Advance Invoice
- Design Fee Invoice
- Milestone Invoice
- Running Bill
- Variation Order Invoice
- Final Invoice
- Credit Note

The invoice type must match the commercial agreement.

---

## Step 3 — Validate Billing Amount

Verify:

- Approved Scope
- Previous Invoices
- Amount Already Received
- Outstanding Balance
- Current Milestone Value
- Approved Variations

Prevent duplicate or incorrect billing.

---

## Step 4 — Calculate Taxes

Apply:

- GST
- Other applicable taxes

Display taxes separately from the taxable amount.

---

## Step 5 — Generate Invoice Details

Include:

- Invoice Number
- Invoice Date
- Due Date
- Client Information
- Project Information
- Description of Services
- Quantity (if applicable)
- Unit Rate
- Taxable Amount
- GST
- Total Amount Payable

Invoice numbering should follow company standards.

---

## Step 6 — Add Payment Information

Include:

- Bank Name
- Account Holder
- Account Number
- IFSC Code
- UPI (if applicable)
- Payment Terms
- Due Date

Ensure payment instructions are accurate and complete.

---

## Step 7 — Validate Compliance

Verify:

- GST Details
- Company Information
- Client Information
- Tax Calculations
- Invoice Number
- Commercial References
- Arithmetic Accuracy

Review before issuing the invoice.

---

## Step 8 — Issue Invoice

Generate a professional invoice containing:

- Company Branding
- Invoice Header
- Client Details
- Project Details
- Billing Summary
- Tax Summary
- Total Amount
- Payment Instructions
- Terms & Conditions

---

## Step 9 — Record Invoice

Update:

- Invoice Register
- Payment Tracker
- Client Ledger
- Outstanding Receivables
- Financial Dashboard

Maintain a complete audit trail.

---

# Standard Invoice Sections

1. Company Information
2. Invoice Details
3. Client Information
4. Project Information
5. Billing Description
6. Tax Summary
7. Grand Total
8. Payment Instructions
9. Terms & Conditions
10. Authorized Signature

---

# Rules

Always:

- Verify quotation references.
- Match billing with project milestones.
- Calculate taxes accurately.
- Maintain sequential invoice numbering.
- Include payment instructions.
- Record issued invoices.
- Follow applicable tax regulations.
- Maintain professional formatting.

Never:

- Generate duplicate invoices.
- Bill unapproved work.
- Modify approved commercial values.
- Omit tax information.
- Use incorrect invoice numbering.
- Issue invoices without verification.

Generate:

- Professional Invoice

- Matches approved commercial documents.
