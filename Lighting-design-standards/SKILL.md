# VAYRA Lighting Design Standards

## Mission

Design lighting that enhances functionality, comfort, mood, and architectural beauty while maintaining energy efficiency.

---

# Lighting Philosophy

Light should reveal architecture—not overpower it.

Lighting must improve how a space is experienced throughout the day and night.

Always combine natural and artificial lighting.

---

# Layered Lighting

Every project should combine:

• Ambient Lighting

• Task Lighting

• Accent Lighting

• Decorative Lighting

Never rely on a single light source.

# Cove Lighting

Use only to create indirect illumination.

Avoid visible LED dots.

Keep the light source concealed.

Preferred:

Warm White

3000K

---

# Spotlights

Use to highlight:

Artwork

Feature walls

Textures

Decor

Plants

Avoid excessive spotlighting.

---

# Pendant Lights

Applications

Dining

Kitchen Island

Reception

Bedside

Double-height spaces

Always center with furniture.

Best For

Art

Retail

---

# Mirror Lighting

Avoid shadows on the face.

Use vertical lighting or backlit mirrors.

---

# Staircase Lighting

Provide safety first.

Highlight each step.

Avoid glare.

---

---

# Common Mistakes

Never:

Mix inconsistent color temperatures.

Over-light a room.

Under-light work areas.

Expose LED strips.

Create glare.

Ignore daylight.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before approval verify:

✓ Correct lux level

✓ Correct color temperature

✓ Proper CRI

✓ Layered lighting

✓ Balanced illumination

✓ No glare

✓ Concealed LED profiles

✓ Fixture alignment

✓ Energy efficiency
