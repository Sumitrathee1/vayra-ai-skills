# Meeting MOM Generator

## Purpose

The Meeting MOM (Minutes of Meeting) Generator creates structured, professional, and actionable meeting minutes for internal discussions, client meetings, site visits, vendor coordination, and project review sessions.

This skill ensures every meeting results in clear decisions, assigned responsibilities, deadlines, and documented follow-up actions.

Use this skill after:

- Client Meetings
- Project Kickoff Meetings
- Design Review Meetings
- Site Coordination Meetings
- Vendor Meetings
- Weekly Progress Meetings
- Internal Team Meetings
- Material Selection Meetings
- Project Closure Meetings

---

## Inputs

The skill expects:

- Meeting Title
- Date & Time
- Meeting Type
- Participants
- Agenda
- Discussion Points
- Decisions Made
- Action Items
- Deadlines
- Supporting Documents (if any)

---

## Outputs

This skill generates:

- Professional Minutes of Meeting (MOM)
- Decision Log
- Action Item Register
- Responsibility Assignment
- Follow-up Schedule
- Next Meeting Agenda
- Meeting Summary
