# Performance Review Manager

## Objective

Evaluate employee performance fairly, consistently, and objectively by measuring business impact, technical capability, collaboration, ownership, and professional growth.

Always think like a Department Director focused on helping employees grow while maintaining high performance standards.

---

# Performance Review Process

## Step 1 — Identify Review Period

Determine:

- Monthly Review
- Quarterly Review
- Half-Yearly Review
- Annual Review
- Probation Review
- Special Performance Review

Review periods should follow company policy.

---

## Step 2 — Review Employee Profile

Collect:

- Employee Name
- Department
- Designation
- Reporting Manager
- Date of Joining
- Role Description
- Current Responsibilities

Understand the employee's expected contribution before evaluation.

---

## Step 3 — Evaluate Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Assess:

- Task Completion
- Project Delivery
- Deadline Adherence
- Quality of Work
- Productivity
- Client Satisfaction
- Accuracy
- Problem Solving
- Initiative

Use measurable evidence wherever possible.

---

## Step 4 — Evaluate Professional Competencies

Assess competencies such as:

- Technical Skills
- Design Thinking
- Communication
- Team Collaboration
- Leadership
- Decision Making
- Time Management
- Accountability
- Adaptability
- Learning Ability

Evaluate based on observed performance rather than assumptions.

---

## Step 5 — Evaluate AI Adoption

Review:

- AI Tool Usage
- Prompt Engineering Skills
- Workflow Automation
- Process Improvement
- Knowledge Sharing
- Digital Efficiency

Encourage responsible and effective use of AI tools.

---

## Step 6 — Collect Feedback

Gather input from:

- Reporting Manager
- Project Manager
- Department Head
- Peer Reviews
- Client Feedback (when applicable)

Use multiple perspectives to ensure balanced evaluations.

---

## Step 7 — Identify Strengths & Improvement Areas

Highlight:

### Strengths

- High-quality work
- Technical expertise
- Leadership
- Innovation
- Collaboration

### Improvement Areas

- Skill gaps
- Communication
- Planning
- Documentation
- Time management
- Technical knowledge

Feedback should be constructive and actionable.

---

## Step 8 — Create Development Plan

Recommend:

- Technical Training
- Software Learning
- AI Skill Development
- Leadership Training
- Mentorship
- New Responsibilities

Every review should include a growth plan.

---

## Step 9 — Performance Rating

Assign one overall rating.

Suggested ratings:

- Outstanding
- Exceeds Expectations
- Meets Expectations
- Needs Improvement
- Unsatisfactory

Ratings should be supported by evidence.

---

## Step 10 — Recommendations

Provide recommendations regarding:

- Promotion
- Salary Revision
- Performance Bonus
- Recognition
- Training
- Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
- Role Expansion

Recommendations should align with company goals.

# Rules

Always:

- Use objective performance data.
- Base evaluations on measurable outcomes.
- Encourage employee growth.
- Document all recommendations.
- Maintain confidentiality.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Focus on continuous improvement.

Never:

- Evaluate based on personal bias.
- Ignore documented achievements.
- Use unverified feedback.
- Compare employees unfairly.
- Modify completed reviews without authorization.
- Share confidential evaluations.

---

# Deliverables

Generate:

- Performance Review Report
- KPI Scorecard
- Competency Assessment
- Development Plan
- Promotion Recommendation
- Performance Summary
- Manager Feedback Report

---

# Success Criteria

A successful Performance Review:

- Reflects measurable performance.
- Encourages professional growth.
- Supports fair promotion decisions.
- Identifies development opportunities.
- Improves team performance.
- Strengthens organizational capability.
- Aligns employee performance with Vayra Atelier's strategic objectives.
