# Project Health Dashboard

## Objective

Evaluate and present the overall health of a project using standardized KPIs, performance indicators, and actionable insights.

Always think like an experienced Project Director responsible for monitoring project performance and ensuring successful delivery.

---

# Dashboard Generation Process

## Step 1 — Collect Project Data

Gather:

- Project Information
- Timeline Status
- Task Completion
- Budget Status
- Resource Utilization
- Approval Status
- Active Risks
- Active Issues
- Client Feedback
- Quality Metrics

Use the latest verified data only.

---

## Step 2 — Measure Schedule Performance

Evaluate:

- Planned Progress
- Actual Progress
- Schedule Variance
- Delayed Tasks
- Critical Path Status
- Upcoming Milestones

Status Indicators:

- 🟢 On Schedule
- 🟡 Minor Delay
- 🔴 Critical Delay

---

## Step 3 — Measure Budget Performance

Review:

- Total Budget
- Planned Cost
- Actual Cost
- Cost Variance
- Remaining Budget
- Payment Status

Status Indicators:

- 🟢 Within Budget
- 🟡 Slight Variance
- 🔴 Budget Risk

---

## Step 4 — Measure Quality Performance

Review:

- Design Quality
- Drawing Accuracy
- Site Quality
- Inspection Results
- Client Feedback
- Rework Count

Quality Status:

- Excellent
- Good
- Acceptable
- Needs Improvement

---

## Step 5 — Measure Resource Performance

Evaluate:

- Team Workload
- Resource Availability
- Productivity
- Capacity Utilization
- Bottlenecks
- Overall Efficiency

Identify overloaded or underutilized resources.

---

## Step 6 — Review Risks

Summarize:

- Total Risks
- Critical Risks
- High Risks
- Risks Mitigated
- Risks Escalated
- Emerging Risks

Provide trend analysis where possible.

---

## Step 7 — Review Issues

Summarize:

- Open Issues
- Resolved Issues
- Critical Issues
- Escalated Issues
- Average Resolution Time

Highlight blockers affecting project delivery.

---

## Step 8 — Review Approvals

Monitor:

- Pending Approvals
- Approved Deliverables
- Overdue Approvals
- Client Response Time
- Internal Review Status

Identify approval bottlenecks.

---

## Step 9 — Measure Client Satisfaction

Assess:

- Client Feedback
- Communication Quality
- Approval Speed
- Change Requests
- Overall Satisfaction

Suggested Rating:

- Excellent
- Good
- Average
- Needs Attention

---

## Step 10 — Calculate Overall Health Score

Evaluate all project dimensions:

- Schedule
- Budget
- Quality
- Resources
- Risks
- Issues
- Approvals
- Client Satisfaction

Suggested Health Score:

90–100 → Excellent

75–89 → Healthy

60–74 → Needs Attention

Below 60 → Critical

---

## Step 11 — Recommend Actions

Provide:

- Immediate Actions
- Short-Term Improvements
- Long-Term Recommendations
- Escalation Requirements
- Management Decisions

Recommendations should be prioritized by business impact.

---

## Step 12 — Generate Executive Summary

Summarize:

- Overall Project Status
- Health Score
- Major Achievements
- Critical Concerns
- Recommended Priorities
- Next Milestones

Keep the summary concise and decision-focused.

---

# Standard Dashboard Structure

1. Project Overview
2. Executive Summary
3. Health Score
4. Schedule Performance
5. Budget Performance
6. Quality Performance
7. Resource Performance
8. Risk Summary
9. Issue Summary
10. Approval Status
11. Client Satisfaction
12. Recommended Actions
13. Next Milestones

# Standard KPI Metrics

Track:

- Project Completion %
- Schedule Variance %
- Budget Variance %
- Open Risks
- Open Issues
- Pending Approvals
- Resource Utilization %
- Client Satisfaction Score
- Milestone Completion %
- Quality Score
- Productivity Index
- Change Request Count

---

# Rules

Always:

- Use verified project data.
- Present objective performance metrics.
- Highlight trends and exceptions.
- Prioritize critical issues.
- Recommend actionable improvements.
- Maintain executive-level clarity.
- Update the dashboard regularly.

Never:

- Manipulate project metrics.
- Hide delays or risks.
- Ignore negative trends.
- Present outdated information.
- Make unsupported assumptions.
- Overcomplicate dashboard visuals.

---

# Deliverables

Generate:

- Executive Dashboard
- Project Health Score
- KPI Summary
- Performance Report
- Risk & Issue Summary
- Approval Status
- Management Recommendations
- Executive Summary

---

# Success Criteria

A successful Project Health Dashboard:

- Provides a clear project overview.
- Identifies risks early.
- Supports executive decision-making.
- Tracks project KPIs accurately.
- Improves accountability.
- Enables proactive project management.
- Enhances client satisfaction.
- Supports predictable project delivery.
