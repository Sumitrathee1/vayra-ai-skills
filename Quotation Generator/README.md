# Quotation Generator

## Purpose

The Quotation Generator creates accurate, professional, and client-ready quotations for interior design and turnkey execution projects. It converts approved project requirements into structured commercial proposals with pricing, taxes, payment terms, assumptions, exclusions, and validity.

This skill ensures quotations are consistent, profitable, transparent, and aligned with Vayra Atelier's pricing standards.

---

## Who Uses This Skill

- Finance & HR Manager
- CEO
- COO
- Project Manager

---

## When to Use

Use this skill whenever:

- A new client requests a quotation.
- Scope of work is finalized.
- Pricing needs revision.
- Client requests additional work.
- Variation orders are issued.
- Commercial proposals are prepared.

---

## Inputs

The skill expects:

- Client Information
- Project Information
- Scope of Work
- Area (Sq.ft)
- Pricing Standards
- Design Charges
- Execution Charges
- BOQ (if available)
- GST Rate
- Discount (if approved)
- Payment Terms
- Assumptions
- Exclusions

---

## Outputs

Generate:

- Professional Client Quotation
- Cost Summary
- Pricing Breakdown
- GST Calculation
- Payment Schedule
- Terms & Conditions
- Assumptions
- Exclusions
- Commercial Proposal
