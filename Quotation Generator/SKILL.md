# Quotation Generator

## Objective

Generate professional, accurate, profitable, and client-friendly quotations that comply with Vayra Atelier's pricing policies and commercial standards.

Always think like a Commercial Manager preparing a quotation that is financially viable, technically accurate, and easy for the client to understand.

---

# Quotation Generation Process

## Step 1 — Understand Project

Collect:

- Client Name
- Project Name
- Project Type
- Site Location
- Total Area
- Design Scope
- Execution Scope
- Deliverables
- Timeline

Never begin pricing without understanding the complete scope.

---

## Step 2 — Define Scope

Clearly identify:

Included Services

Examples:

- Interior Design
- Furniture Layout
- 3D Visualization
- Working Drawings
- BOQ
- Material Selection
- Site Coordination
- Execution

Clearly separate included and excluded work.

---

## Step 3 — Calculate Pricing

Calculate pricing using approved company standards.

Include:

- Design Charges
- Execution Charges
- Consultation Charges
- Site Visit Charges
- Additional Services
- Custom Requirements

Never use unofficial pricing.

---

## Step 4 — Apply Taxes

Calculate:

- GST
- Other applicable taxes

Display taxes separately from base pricing.

---

## Step 5 — Prepare Payment Schedule

Define payment milestones.

Example:

- Booking Amount
- Design Stage
- Drawing Stage
- Execution Stage
- Handover

Ensure payment terms align with company policy.

---

## Step 6 — Define Assumptions

Clearly mention assumptions such as:

- Site measurements provided by client.
- Civil work excluded unless specified.
- Pricing valid for limited duration.
- Material prices subject to market variation.

Assumptions must reduce commercial ambiguity.

---

## Step 7 — Define Exclusions

List exclusions such as:

- Government Fees
- Structural Changes
- Appliances
- Loose Furniture
- Electrical Load Upgrades
- Third-party Consultant Fees

Never leave exclusions undefined.

---

## Step 8 — Commercial Review

Verify:

- Scope completeness
- Pricing accuracy
- Profit margin
- GST calculation
- Discount approval
- Payment terms
- Terms & Conditions

Review before issuing.

---

## Step 9 — Generate Final Quotation

Include:

- Company Details
- Client Details
- Project Information
- Scope
- Pricing Table
- GST
- Grand Total
- Payment Schedule
- Assumptions
- Exclusions
- Terms & Conditions
- Validity Period
- Authorized Signature

# Rules

Always:

- Follow approved pricing standards.
- Calculate taxes accurately.
- Use professional formatting.
- Clearly define scope.
- Clearly define exclusions.
- Include payment terms.
- Mention quotation validity.
- Maintain commercial transparency.

Never:

- Guess pricing.
- Apply unauthorized discounts.
- Hide taxes.
- Omit exclusions.
- Use expired pricing standards.
- Modify approved commercial policies.
