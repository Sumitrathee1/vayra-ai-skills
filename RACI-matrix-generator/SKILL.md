# RACI Matrix Generator

## Objective

Create a Responsibility Assignment Matrix (RACI) for every project task to ensure clear ownership, accountability, consultation, and communication throughout the project lifecycle.

Always think like an experienced Project Manager responsible for building efficient, transparent, and collaborative teams.

---

# RACI Definitions

## Responsible (R)

The person who performs the work.

Every task must have at least one Responsible person.

---

## Accountable (A)

The person who owns the final outcome and approves the completed work.

Every task must have only one Accountable person.

---

## Consulted (C)

People who provide expertise, technical input, or recommendations before work is completed.

Consultation should occur before important decisions are finalized.

---

## Informed (I)

People who need updates on progress or completion but are not directly involved in execution.

---

# Responsibility Assignment Process

## Step 1 — Review Project Information

Understand:

- Project Scope
- Work Breakdown Structure
- Timeline
- Team Structure
- Client Requirements
- Approval Workflow

---

## Step 2 — Identify All Tasks

List every:

- Phase
- Deliverable
- Major Task
- Subtask
- Approval
- Review Activity

Nothing should be left without ownership.

---

## Step 3 — Assign Responsible (R)

Identify the individual or role responsible for executing each task.

Responsible roles may include:

- Project Manager
- SID
- Draftsman
- 3D Visualizer
- Procurement
- Finance
- HR

---

## Step 4 — Assign Accountable (A)

Assign one person who is ultimately answerable for the successful completion of each task.

Only one Accountable role is permitted per task.

---

## Step 5 — Assign Consulted (C)

Identify individuals or departments whose expertise is required before completing the task.

Examples:

- CDO
- Structural Consultant
- MEP Consultant
- Client
- Vendor

---

## Step 6 — Assign Informed (I)

Identify stakeholders who should receive updates after progress or completion.

Examples:

- CEO
- COO
- Client
- Finance
- HR

---

## Step 7 — Validate Matrix

Ensure:

- Every task has one Accountable role.
- Every task has at least one Responsible role.
- Consultation is meaningful.
- Stakeholders are informed appropriately.
- No role is overloaded unnecessarily.

---

# RACI Guidelines

Always:

- Assign one Accountable owner.
- Keep responsibilities clear.
- Match responsibilities to expertise.
- Update the matrix when scope changes.
- Review responsibilities during project kickoff.

Never:

- Assign multiple Accountable owners.
- Leave tasks without ownership.
- Assign responsibility to unavailable resources.
- Duplicate responsibilities unnecessarily.
- Ignore stakeholder communication.
