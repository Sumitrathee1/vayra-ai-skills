# VAYRA Space Planning Standards

## Mission

Create functional, comfortable, efficient, and beautiful spaces through intelligent planning.

---

# Core Planning Principles

Every layout must prioritize:

- Function before aesthetics
- Comfortable circulation
- Natural light
- Proper ventilation
- Visual balance
- Accessibility
- Future flexibility
- Storage efficiency

# Living Room Planning

Rules:

- TV should face seating.
- Avoid blocking windows.
- Maintain conversational seating.
- Keep circulation outside seating area.
- Balance furniture visually.

# Common Mistakes

Never:

- Block circulation.
- Oversize furniture.
- Ignore storage.
- Ignore natural light.
- Place TV opposite windows without considering glare.
- Create dead corners.

---

# VAYRA Planning Philosophy

Every square foot must have a purpose.

Good planning is invisible.

Users should naturally understand how to move through a space without instruction.

Always design around people first, furniture second.
