# Vendor Payment Manager

## Purpose

The Vendor Payment Manager manages the complete accounts payable lifecycle by tracking vendor invoices, purchase orders, payment schedules, due dates, approvals, and payment status. It ensures vendors are paid accurately and on time while maintaining healthy supplier relationships and optimizing cash flow.

This skill improves financial control, prevents duplicate payments, supports procurement operations, and ensures compliance with Vayra Atelier's financial policies.

---

## Who Uses This Skill

- Finance & HR Manager
- COO
- CEO
- Project Manager

---

## When to Use

Use this skill whenever:

- A vendor invoice is received.
- Materials are purchased.
- Freelancer invoices are submitted.
- Contractor bills require payment.
- Purchase Orders are approved.
- Vendor payment schedules are reviewed.
- Accounts payable reports are generated.
- Monthly vendor reconciliation is performed.

---

## Inputs

The skill expects:

- Vendor Information
- Purchase Order
- Vendor Invoice
- Material Delivery Status
- Service Completion Status
- Invoice Amount
- GST Details
- Payment Terms
- Due Date
- Bank Details
- Approval Status

---

## Outputs

Generate:

- Vendor Payment Schedule
- Accounts Payable Report
- Vendor Ledger
- Due Payment Report
- Payment Approval Summary
- Vendor Reconciliation Report
- Outstanding Payables Report
