# Workforce Dashboard

## Objective

Provide a unified operational view of the entire workforce by combining human resources, AI agents, organizational performance, resource utilization, and business insights into one executive dashboard.

Always think like a Chief Operations Officer responsible for maximizing organizational performance.

---

# Dashboard Generation Process

## Step 1 — Workforce Overview

Summarize:

### Human Workforce

- Total Employees
- Active Employees
- Departments
- Leadership Team
- New Joiners
- Employees on Notice

### AI Workforce

- Total AI Agents
- Active Agents
- Agents Under Maintenance
- Retired Agents
- Newly Deployed Agents

Provide a high-level organizational snapshot.

---

## Step 2 — Department Performance

Review each department:

- Design
- Visualization
- Technical
- Project Management
- Finance
- Marketing
- Sales
- Workforce Operations

Display:

- Department KPIs
- Productivity
- Current Workload
- Resource Availability

---

## Step 3 — Human Workforce Metrics

Monitor:

- Performance Ratings
- Productivity
- Skills Distribution
- Promotions
- Payroll Status
- Employee Growth

Identify workforce strengths and development opportunities.

---

## Step 4 — AI Workforce Metrics

Monitor:

- Active Agents
- Tasks Completed
- Success Rate
- Response Time
- Token Consumption
- AI Operating Cost
- Skill Coverage
- Knowledge Currency
- Version Compliance

Track AI workforce health continuously.

---

## Step 5 — Resource Utilization

Analyze:

- Human Capacity
- AI Capacity
- Project Allocation
- Team Utilization
- Available Resources
- Resource Conflicts

Recommend workload balancing where necessary.

---

## Step 6 — Organizational KPIs

Display:

- Overall Productivity
- Project Completion Rate
- Client Satisfaction
- Automation Coverage
- Operational Efficiency
- Cost Efficiency
- Quality Score

Compare results against organizational targets.

---

## Step 7 — Workforce Risks

Identify:

- Resource Shortages
- Skill Gaps
- AI Governance Issues
- High Workloads
- Single Points of Failure
- Critical Dependencies
- Compliance Risks

Prioritize risks by business impact.

---

## Step 8 — Strategic Recommendations

Recommend actions such as:

### Human Workforce

- Hiring
- Promotions
- Training
- Resource Reallocation

### AI Workforce

- Skill Installation
- Prompt Optimization
- Knowledge Updates
- Agent Upgrades
- Workflow Automation

Recommendations should be prioritized by expected business value.

---

## Step 9 — Executive Summary

Generate:

### Human Workforce

- Headcount
- Productivity
- Performance Trend

### AI Workforce

- AI Health
- Automation Coverage
- Operational Cost

### Business

- Overall Workforce Health
- Key Achievements
- Critical Issues
- Recommended Actions

Provide a concise summary for leadership.

# Rules

Always:

- Use the latest approved workforce data.
- Combine human and AI metrics consistently.
- Present KPIs visually where possible.
- Highlight critical risks.
- Include actionable recommendations.
- Preserve historical trends.
- Maintain executive-level clarity.

Never:

- Mix outdated and current data.
- Display confidential payroll details in public dashboards.
- Ignore governance issues.
- Omit significant operational risks.
- Generate dashboards without verified data.

# Success Criteria

A successful Workforce Dashboard:

- Provides a complete view of the organization's workforce.
- Combines human and AI operational metrics.
- Enables data-driven executive decisions.
- Identifies risks and opportunities early.
- Supports resource optimization.
- Tracks organizational performance over time.
- Aligns workforce strategy with Vayra Atelier's business objectives.
