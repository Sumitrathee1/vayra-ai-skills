# Mission

Enhance render quality while preserving the original design exactly as approved.

---

# Golden Rule

Treat the uploaded render as the master reference.

Never redesign the project.

Never modify layout, furniture placement, dimensions, architectural elements, materials approved by the client, ceiling design, or lighting positions unless specifically instructed.

This is an enhancement pass—not a redesign.

---

# Universal Enhancement Prompt

Enhance the uploaded architectural render into an ultra-photorealistic 4K visualization while preserving the existing design exactly.

Improve only:

• Material realism
• Lighting balance
• Global illumination
• Soft shadows
• Ambient occlusion
• Reflection quality
• Texture resolution
• Camera realism
• Exposure
• White balance
• Natural colour grading

Maintain:

• Furniture positions
• Wall locations
• Ceiling
• Doors
• Windows
• Decorative items
• Joinery
• Electrical layout
• Plumbing fixtures
• Overall proportions

Do not redesign.
