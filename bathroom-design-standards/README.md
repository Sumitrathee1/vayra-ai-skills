# VAYRA Bathroom Design Standards

Official bathroom planning and design standards for VAYRA Atelier.

This skill provides design principles, ergonomic standards, plumbing coordination, waterproofing guidelines, ventilation strategies, material recommendations, lighting standards, and quality control measures for residential and commercial bathrooms.

## Purpose

Design safe, hygienic, functional, and premium bathrooms that are easy to maintain and technically sound.

## Covers

- Space Planning
- Wet & Dry Zones
- Plumbing
- Waterproofing
- Fixtures
- Ventilation
- Lighting
- Materials
- Storage
- Accessibility
- Quality Checklist
