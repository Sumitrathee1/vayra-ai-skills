# VAYRA Bathroom Design Standards

## Mission

Design bathrooms that are functional, hygienic, comfortable, durable, and visually elegant.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every bathroom should prioritize:

- Safety
- Hygiene
- Easy maintenance
- Water efficiency
- Comfort
- Premium aesthetics
- Long-term durability

---

# Space Planning

Separate wet and dry areas wherever possible.

Maintain clear circulation around fixtures.

Avoid door conflicts with sanitary fixtures.

---

# Recommended Dimensions

Minimum Bathroom

1500 × 2100 mm

Comfort Bathroom

1800 × 2400 mm

Luxury Bathroom

2400 × 3000 mm or larger

---

# Shower Area

Minimum

900 × 900 mm

Recommended

1200 × 900 mm

Luxury

1500 × 900 mm

Use frameless toughened glass partitions where appropriate.

---

# Wash Basin

Counter Height

850–900 mm

Minimum Counter Depth

500 mm

Recommended Counter Depth

550–600 mm

---

# WC Placement

Provide comfortable side clearances.

Maintain minimum front clearance of 750 mm.

Wall-hung WCs preferred where appropriate.

---

# Ventilation

Provide:

- Natural ventilation whenever possible.
- Exhaust fan if natural ventilation is inadequate.
- Proper air circulation to reduce moisture.

---

# Waterproofing

Waterproof:

- Floor
- Shower walls
- Wet area walls
- Pipe penetrations

Conduct a water ponding test before tiling.

---

# Floor Slopes

Maintain proper slope towards floor drain.

Avoid water stagnation.

---

# Lighting

Layer lighting:

- Ambient
- Mirror Lighting
- Shower Lighting
- Accent Lighting (optional)

Preferred Color Temperature

3000K–4000K

CRI 90+

---

# Materials

Preferred Wall Finish

- Large-format porcelain tiles
- Natural stone (where suitable)

Preferred Floor Finish

- Anti-skid porcelain tiles

Countertops

- Quartz
- Granite
- Sintered Stone

Avoid polished flooring in wet zones.

---

# Storage

Include:

- Vanity storage
- Mirror cabinet
- Shower niche
- Linen storage (if space allows)

---

# Electrical Planning

Provide:

- GFCI/RCD-protected sockets (as per local code)
- Mirror light
- Exhaust fan
- Geyser point
- Heated mirror provision (optional)

Keep electrical fittings away from direct water exposure.

---

# Plumbing Coordination

Plan:

- Hot and cold water lines
- Floor drains
- Trap access
- Maintenance access
- Fixture alignment

Coordinate plumbing before finalizing tiles.

---

# Accessibility

Where required:

- Grab bars
- Curbless shower
- Wider door
- Wheelchair turning radius

---

# Common Mistakes

Never:

- Ignore waterproofing.
- Provide insufficient ventilation.
- Use slippery flooring.
- Place electrical fittings in wet zones.
- Misalign fixtures.
- Create poor floor slopes.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before approval verify:

✓ Waterproofing

✓ Drainage

✓ Ventilation

✓ Lighting

✓ Plumbing coordination

✓ Fixture spacing

✓ Material suitability

✓ Storage

✓ Buildability

✓ Maintenance
