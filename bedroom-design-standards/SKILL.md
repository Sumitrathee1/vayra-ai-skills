# VAYRA Bedroom Design Standards

## Mission

Design bedrooms that promote comfort, relaxation, functionality, and timeless elegance.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every bedroom should provide:

- Comfort
- Privacy
- Functionality
- Efficient storage
- Relaxing ambience
- Premium aesthetics
- Easy maintenance

---

# Space Planning

Always maintain:

Minimum circulation beside bed

750 mm

Preferred

900 mm

Minimum circulation in front of wardrobe

900 mm

Preferred

1200 mm

Avoid blocked circulation.

---

# Bed Planning

Preferred Sizes

Single

900 × 2000 mm

Queen

1500 × 2000 mm

King

1800 × 2000 mm

Super King

2000 × 2000 mm

Place the headboard against a solid wall whenever possible.

Avoid placing the bed directly under a window unless justified by the design.

---

# Side Tables

Recommended Width

450–600 mm

Height

Flush with mattress height.

Provide charging points on both sides.

---

# Wardrobes

Preferred Depth

600 mm

Internal Layout

- Long Hanging
- Short Hanging
- Drawers
- Shelves
- Accessories
- Loft Storage

Provide soft-close hardware.

---

# Study Area

Recommended Desk Height

750 mm

Provide:

Task lighting

Charging points

Storage

Comfortable seating

Natural light where possible.

---

# TV Unit

Mount TV at seated eye level.

Provide concealed cable management.

Ensure adequate viewing distance.

---

# Lighting

Layer lighting:

Ambient

Task

Accent

Decorative

Recommended Color Temperature

2700K–3000K

Use dimmable bedside lighting where appropriate.

---

# False Ceiling

Keep ceiling simple.

Integrate indirect cove lighting.

Avoid excessive design complexity.

---

# Materials

Preferred

- HDHMR
- BWR Plywood
- Laminate
- Veneer
- Fabric Panels
- Quartz for dressing counters

Use durable finishes suitable for daily use.

---

# Color Palette

Preferred

Warm White

Beige

Greige

Oak

Walnut

Muted Greens

Soft Grey

Introduce accent colours only where appropriate.

---

# Electrical Planning

Provide:

- Bedside switches
- USB/charging points
- TV point
- Internet point
- Dressing light
- Study sockets
- AC point

Plan furniture and electrical layouts together.

---

# Storage

Include:

Wardrobe

Bed storage (if required)

Side table storage

Study storage

Loft (where appropriate)

Avoid unnecessary clutter.

---

# Vastu Considerations (Optional)

If requested by the client:

- Prefer headboard towards the south or west wall.
- Avoid mirrors directly reflecting the bed.
- Maintain a balanced room layout.

Treat Vastu as a client preference, not a mandatory design rule.

---

# Common Mistakes

Never:

- Oversize furniture.
- Block circulation.
- Ignore storage.
- Under-light the room.
- Place switches inconveniently.
- Ignore cable management.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before approval verify:

✓ Comfortable circulation

✓ Proper bed placement

✓ Functional wardrobe

✓ Balanced lighting

✓ Adequate storage

✓ Furniture proportions

✓ Electrical coordination

✓ Material suitability

✓ Buildability

✓ Client lifestyle requirements
