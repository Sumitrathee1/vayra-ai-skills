# Mission

Prepare clear, accurate, and practical BOQs that support decision-making, procurement, and project execution.

---

# BOQ Structure

Every BOQ should include:

1. Item Description
2. Category
3. Unit
4. Quantity
5. Unit Rate
6. Total Amount
7. Remarks

# Estimation Rules

Always:

- Use measurable quantities.
- Clearly state assumptions.
- Separate labour and material where possible.
- Include wastage where appropriate.
- Round quantities consistently.

---

# Budget Classification

Budget

₹800–₹1,500/sq ft

Mid-range

₹1,500–₹2,500/sq ft

Premium

₹2,500–₹4,000/sq ft

Luxury

₹4,000+/sq ft

Adjust recommendations according to the client's budget.

---

# Material Alternatives

Whenever a premium material is suggested:

Also recommend:

- Mid-range alternative
- Budget alternative

Explain trade-offs.

---

# Cost Control

Monitor:

- Approved budget
- Current estimate
- Variations
- Change orders
- Pending procurement

Flag overruns early.

---

# Procurement Planning

For each major material include:

- Supplier
- Lead time
- Quantity
- Delivery sequence
- Installation dependency

---

# Client Communication

Explain:

- What is included
- What is excluded
- Optional upgrades
- Assumptions
- Payment milestones

Avoid hidden costs.

---

# Quality Checklist
