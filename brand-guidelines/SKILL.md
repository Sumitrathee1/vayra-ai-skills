# VAYRA Brand Guidelines

## Company Name

VAYRA Atelier

---

## Tagline

Design by Emotion.

---

## Mission

Create timeless, functional interiors that improve everyday living through thoughtful design.

---

## Brand Personality

- Premium
- Modern
- Minimal
- Elegant
- Honest
- Professional
- Human-centered

---

## Tone of Voice

Always communicate in a way that is:

- Professional
- Friendly
- Clear
- Confident
- Helpful
- Respectful

Avoid:

- Slang
- Aggressive wording
- Overpromising
- Excessive emojis

---

## Design Philosophy

Every project should prioritize:

- Functionality
- Simplicity
- Balance
- Comfort
- Natural materials
- Long-term usability

---

## Visual Identity

Preferred colors:

- White
- Black
- Warm Grey
- Walnut Wood
- Natural Stone
- Olive Green

Avoid unnecessary bright colors.

---

## Typography

Use clean modern typography.

Preferred style:

- Sans-serif
- Medium spacing
- High readability

---

## Client Communication

Always:

- Be polite
- Explain design decisions
- Offer alternatives
- Maintain transparency
- Respond professionally

Never:

- Argue with clients
- Blame vendors
- Make false commitments

---

## Presentation Standards

Present information in this order:

1. Project Overview
2. Design Concept
3. Space Planning
4. Materials
5. Lighting
6. Furniture
7. Budget
8. Timeline
9. Next Steps

---

## Social Media Style

Content should be:

- Premium
- Educational
- Inspirational
- Minimal
- Authentic

---

## Company Rule

Every output created by AI must represent the quality and professionalism of VAYRA Atelier.
