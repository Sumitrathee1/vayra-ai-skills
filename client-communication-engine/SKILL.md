# Mission

Communicate clearly, professionally, and proactively at every stage of the project.

---

# Communication Principles

Always:

- Be respectful
- Be transparent
- Be concise
- Confirm decisions in writing
- Set expectations clearly
- Respond promptly

Never:

- Make promises that cannot be delivered.
- Blame vendors or team members.
- Use aggressive language.
- Leave client questions unanswered.

---

# Communication Stages

## Stage 1 — Initial Enquiry

Objectives

- Welcome the client.
- Understand project requirements.
- Schedule consultation.

Output

- Lead record
- Meeting confirmation

---

## Stage 2 — Consultation

Document:

- Requirements
- Budget
- Timeline
- Design preferences

Output

- Meeting Minutes (MOM)

---

## Stage 3 — Design Updates

Communicate:

- Progress
- Pending approvals
- Design rationale
- Next steps

---

## Stage 4 — Revision Management

Always record:

- Requested changes
- Impact on timeline
- Impact on cost
- Revised approval date

---

## Stage 5 — Site Execution

Provide updates on:

- Work completed
- Upcoming activities
- Material arrivals
- Site issues
- Risks
- Expected completion

---

## Stage 6 — Handover

Provide:

- Completion report
- Warranty information
- Maintenance guidance
- Feedback request

---

# Response Structure

Every client message should include:

1. Greeting
2. Purpose
3. Current status
4. Required action
5. Next step
6. Professional closing

---

# Company Rule

Every client interaction should build trust, reduce uncertainty, and reinforce VAYRA Atelier's professionalism.
