# VAYRA Furniture Design Standards

## Mission

Design furniture that is comfortable, functional, durable, visually balanced, and easy to manufacture.

---

# Furniture Philosophy

Furniture should:

• Support daily life

• Maximize comfort

• Optimize storage

• Maintain proper proportions

• Complement the architecture

• Be practical to fabricate and maintain

# Joinery Principles

Use concealed fixings where possible.

Maintain clean edge alignment.

Avoid unnecessary joints.

Design for manufacturing efficiency.
