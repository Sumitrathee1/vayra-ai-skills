# VAYRA Living Room Design Standards

Official living room planning and design standards for VAYRA Atelier.

This skill defines furniture layouts, circulation, TV viewing, lighting, material selection, storage, and styling principles for premium residential living rooms.

## Purpose

Design elegant, comfortable, and functional living rooms that enhance family interaction, entertainment, and daily living while maintaining VAYRA Atelier's premium design philosophy.

## Covers

- Space Planning
- Furniture Layout
- TV Wall Design
- Seating Ergonomics
- Lighting
- Material Selection
- False Ceiling
- Styling
- Electrical Planning
- Quality Checklist
