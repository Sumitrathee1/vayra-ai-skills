# VAYRA Living Room Design Standards

## Mission

Create living rooms that are inviting, functional, elegant, and designed around how people gather, relax, and entertain.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every living room should prioritize:

- Comfortable conversation
- Clear circulation
- Natural light
- Balanced proportions
- Functional furniture
- Premium appearance

---

# Space Planning

Maintain:

Main circulation

Minimum 900 mm

Preferred 1200 mm

Avoid placing furniture directly in circulation paths.

---

# Sofa Planning

Recommended Seat Height

420–450 mm

Seat Depth

500–550 mm

Overall Depth

850–950 mm

Arrange seating to encourage conversation.

---

# Coffee Table

Height

350–450 mm

Distance from Sofa

450 mm

Provide easy access without obstructing movement.

---

# TV Unit

TV Centre Height

1000–1100 mm from finished floor (adjust to seating eye level)

Provide:

- Cable management
- Ventilation
- Hidden wiring
- Storage

---

# TV Viewing Distance

55"

2.1–2.8 m

65"

2.5–3.2 m

75"

3.0–3.8 m

---

# Accent Wall

Use premium finishes such as:

- Veneer
- Stone
- Fluted panels
- Textured paint
- Large-format porcelain
- Decorative laminates

Avoid excessive visual clutter.

---

# Lighting

Layer lighting:

- Ambient
- Accent
- Task
- Decorative

Preferred Colour Temperature

3000K

CRI 90+

Include dimming where appropriate.

---

# False Ceiling

Keep the design clean and functional.

Use indirect cove lighting where appropriate.

Coordinate with AC vents and structural elements.

---

# Materials

Preferred

- Veneer
- HDHMR
- BWR Plywood
- Quartz
- Natural Stone
- Textured Paint

---

# Electrical Planning

Provide:

- TV points
- Internet point
- Speaker provision
- Charging outlets
- Floor sockets (where required)
- Decorative lighting controls

---

# Storage

Include where appropriate:

- TV console
- Display shelves
- Closed storage
- Bookshelves
- Decorative niches

Balance display and concealed storage.

---

# Styling

Use:

- Artwork
- Indoor plants
- Rugs
- Cushions
- Decorative lighting
- Sculptures

Keep styling intentional and uncluttered.

---

# Common Mistakes

Never:

- Oversize furniture.
- Mount TV too high.
- Block windows.
- Ignore cable management.
- Overcomplicate ceiling designs.
- Overdecorate.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before approval verify:

✓ Comfortable seating

✓ Proper TV viewing distance

✓ Clear circulation

✓ Balanced lighting

✓ Functional storage

✓ Electrical coordination

✓ Material suitability

✓ Buildability

✓ Client lifestyle alignment

---

# Company Rule

A VAYRA living room should feel welcoming, timeless, and effortlessly functional while reflecting the personality of its occupants.
