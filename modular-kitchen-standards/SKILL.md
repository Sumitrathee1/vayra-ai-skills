# VAYRA Modular Kitchen Design Standards

## Mission

Create kitchens that are functional, ergonomic, durable, hygienic, and visually elegant.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every kitchen should prioritize:

- Functionality
- Workflow efficiency
- Safety
- Storage
- Easy maintenance
- Premium appearance

---

# Kitchen Workflow

Always organize the kitchen around:

1. Storage
2. Preparation
3. Cooking
4. Cleaning

Maintain an efficient work triangle.

---

# Recommended Work Triangle

Maximum perimeter:

7 m

Avoid obstacles between work zones.

---

# Preferred Layouts

- Straight Kitchen
- Parallel Kitchen
- L-Shaped Kitchen
- U-Shaped Kitchen
- Island Kitchen
- Peninsula Kitchen

Select based on room size and circulation.

# Appliance Integration

Provide dedicated space for:

- Refrigerator
- Hob
- Chimney
- Microwave
- Oven
- Dishwasher
- Water Purifier
- Washing Machine (if applicable)

Ensure proper ventilation clearances.

---

# Sink Planning

Recommended Width

600–900 mm

Position near preparation zone.

Provide adequate drainage slope.

---

# Lighting

Layer lighting:

- Ambient
- Task
- Accent

Use under-cabinet lighting for worktops.

Preferred Color Temperature

3000K–3500K

CRI 90+

# Ventilation

Provide:

---

# Safety

Separate electrical and plumbing lines.

Avoid locating electrical outlets directly behind wet areas.

Ensure heat-resistant materials near cooking appliances.

---

# Common Mistakes

Never:

- Block the work triangle.
- Use poor-quality hardware.
- Ignore ventilation.
- Underestimate storage.
- Place the sink too far from the preparation zone.
- Ignore appliance clearances.
