# VAYRA Render Quality Standards

## Mission

Produce photorealistic architectural renders that communicate the design accurately and inspire client confidence.

---

# Core Philosophy

Rendering should enhance reality.

Never redesign approved architecture.

Improve presentation without changing layout or design intent.

---

# Image Quality

Minimum Resolution

3840 × 2160 (4K)

Preferred

7680 × 4320 (8K)

Aspect Ratio

16:9

Use high-quality anti-aliasing.

---

# Camera Standards

Eye Height

1500–1600 mm

Use two-point perspective for interiors.

Avoid excessive wide-angle distortion.

Focus on architectural composition.

---

# Lighting Standards

Use layered lighting:

- Natural daylight
- Ambient lighting
- Task lighting
- Accent lighting

Preferred colour temperature:

3000K

Balance daylight and artificial lighting.

Avoid blown highlights and overly dark shadows.

---

# Material Standards

Every material should have:

- Correct scale
- Realistic roughness
- Accurate reflections
- Surface imperfections where appropriate
- High-resolution textures

Avoid plastic-looking finishes.

---

# Glass

Use realistic reflections and transparency.

Avoid perfectly clean glass unless intentionally specified.

---

# Wood

Maintain realistic grain direction.

Ensure proper texture scale.

Avoid repeating patterns.

---

# Stone

Use high-resolution textures.

Maintain realistic veining.

Avoid stretched UV mapping.

---

# Camera Composition

Apply:

- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines
- Balanced framing
- Natural focal point

Avoid unnecessary empty areas.

---

# AI Enhancement

Enhance only:

- Lighting
- Material realism
- Sharpness
- Color balance
- Atmospheric quality

Never:

- Change furniture
- Change layout
- Add or remove architectural elements
- Alter client-approved materials

---

# Post Production

Adjust only:

- Exposure
- White balance
- Contrast
- Highlights
- Shadows
- Color balance
- Sharpness

Avoid excessive filters.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before delivery verify:

✓ Correct design

✓ Correct materials

✓ Proper lighting

✓ Realistic reflections

✓ Sharp image

✓ Natural colors

✓ Correct camera

✓ No visible rendering artifacts

✓ No floating objects

✓ No texture stretching

✓ 4K minimum output

---

# Company Rule

Every VAYRA render should be convincing enough that a client can confidently imagine the completed space.
