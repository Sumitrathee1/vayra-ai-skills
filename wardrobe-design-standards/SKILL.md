# VAYRA Wardrobe Design Standards

## Mission

Design wardrobes that provide organized storage, effortless usability, premium aesthetics, and long-term durability.

---

# Design Philosophy

Every wardrobe should prioritize:

- Storage efficiency
- Easy access
- Clean aesthetics
- Ergonomics
- Durability
- Future flexibility

---

# Standard Dimensions

Wardrobe Depth

600 mm

Minimum Depth

550 mm

Standard Height

2400 mm

Maximum Practical Height

2700 mm

Loft Height

450–600 mm

---

# Internal Planning

Every wardrobe should include a combination of:

- Long Hanging
- Short Hanging
- Folded Storage
- Drawers
- Accessories
- Loft Storage

Design based on the client's lifestyle and clothing habits.

---

# Hanging Sections

Long Hanging

1500–1700 mm

Short Hanging

900–1100 mm

Trouser Section

800–900 mm

---

# Shelves

Recommended Spacing

300–400 mm

Adjustable shelves preferred where practical.

---

# Drawers

Standard Height

150–250 mm

Top drawer for accessories.

Soft-close runners recommended.

---

# Shoe Storage

Shelf Depth

300–350 mm

Provide ventilation where possible.

---

# Accessories

Include where required:

- Tie rack
- Belt rack
- Jewellery tray
- Pull-out basket
- Laundry basket
- Watch organizer
- Trouser pull-out

---

# Mirror Planning

Options:

- External mirror
- Internal mirror
- Dressing mirror

Avoid obstructing circulation.

---

# Lighting

Provide:

- LED profile lighting
- Motion sensor lighting (optional)
- Internal shelf lighting

Preferred Color Temperature

3000K

CRI 90+

---

# Materials

Preferred

- HDHMR
- BWR Plywood
- Laminates
- Veneer
- Acrylic
- PU Finish

Avoid moisture-sensitive materials in humid environments.

---

# Hardware

Preferred Brands

- Blum
- Hettich
- Hafele
- Grass
- Ebco

Use soft-close hinges and runners.

---

# Sliding Wardrobes

Provide sufficient overlap.

Ensure smooth track operation.

Allow maintenance access.

---

# Hinged Wardrobes

Door Width

450–600 mm

Avoid excessively wide shutters.

---

# Common Mistakes

Never:

- Ignore hanging requirements.
- Design fixed shelves only.
- Oversize shutters.
- Ignore lighting.
- Compromise hardware quality.
- Ignore future storage needs.

---

# Quality Checklist

Before approval verify:

✓ Hanging space

✓ Drawer layout

✓ Shelf spacing

✓ Hardware

✓ Materials

✓ Lighting

✓ Mirror placement

✓ Fabrication feasibility

✓ Client requirements

Every wardrobe should feel effortless to use while remaining elegant, durable, and highly organized.
